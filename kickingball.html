This an Animated Video of an Boy Kicking a Ball
Notes
  • Using my character I made the following with lesson 4: character design, which dealt with the oval primitive tool and square primitive tool.
  • Chopped the upper and lower part of each arm and leg to convert into different symbols.
  • Next I used the rectangle primitive tool, to recreate the upper parts for each leg and arm.
  • Then I used the bone tool and connected each arm, leg and torso.
  • When I used the bone tool I gave it the motion of kicking.
  • Then I made another layer and created a ball, next I used the motion tool to give it the ability to bounce around the stage.
  • Then I created a shadow by using the free transform tool and put the anchor point down, I brought it all down.
  • I went to the farme in properties and made the quality medium, I made the shadow black.
  • I also repeated the process for the ball.
  • Next I used a cartoon background I got off of google.
